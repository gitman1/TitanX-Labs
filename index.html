Weather-Resistant Frame

Engineered for operational excellence in adverse atmospheric conditions. Advanced composite materials and aerodynamic design ensure stable flight through turbulence, precipitation, and high-altitude weather systems.

Up to 3+ hours of continuous autonomous flight per mission. Intelligent battery management, route optimization, and return-to-base protocols ensure mission completion and asset safety.
